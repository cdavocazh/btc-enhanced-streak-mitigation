Root Cause Analysis - The 13-Trade Streak

  • Market Condition: BTC was consolidating after a major rally to $73k ATH. Price oscillated in a $13k range (19% swings) for a month.
  • Positioning Signal Failure: Top traders remained net long throughout (score > 0.4), but price kept making lower highs and lower lows within the range.
  • ATR Problem: ATR was elevated from the March rally (~$1,200), so stops were set $2,160 below entry. However, intraday swings were regularly $3,000-$5,000.
  • RSI Trap: RSI kept hitting the 20-45 "pullback zone" during each dip, triggering new entries that got stopped out on the next dip.

Root Cause Categories

45% Choppy Range-Bound Markets

Pattern: Price oscillates in a wide range (10-20%) with multiple false breakouts. Positioning stays bullish, RSI repeatedly hits entry zone.

Affected Streaks: Mar-Apr 2024 (13 trades), Jan 2024 (4 trades), Nov-Dec 2024 (multiple 3-4 trade streaks)

Why Current Indicators Fail:

  • RSI pullback filter triggers on each dip within the range
  • Positioning score remains positive even in range-bound conditions
  • No mechanism to detect "range" vs "trend" market structure
30% Sharp Trend Reversals

Pattern: Market transitions from uptrend to downtrend. Price falls through SMA200 but entries occur before the cross.

Affected Streaks: Jun-Jul 2024 (8 trades), Oct-Nov 2025 (5 trades), Feb-Mar 2025 (4 trades)

Why Current Indicators Fail:

  • SMA200 is a lagging indicator - doesn't catch trend reversals in time
  • Positioning data lags by 5-15 minutes and top traders may have already reduced exposure
  • No momentum confirmation (e.g., price making lower highs)
25% Low Volatility Compression

Pattern: ATR is elevated from recent volatility, but current price action is much quieter. Stops are too wide relative to actual price movement.

Affected Streaks: Jan 2025 (multiple streaks), Dec 2024-Jan 2025 (3-4 trade streaks)

Why Current Indicators Fail:

  • ATR uses 14-period lookback which includes past volatile periods
  • Stops set at 1.8x ATR may be $1,500-$2,000 when actual range is $500
  • Normal market noise triggers stops that are "right" by ATR but "wrong" by current volatility

Adaptive Indicator Recommendations

Solution 1: Market Regime Detection

Purpose: Distinguish between trending and range-bound markets to avoid the 45% of streaks caused by choppy conditions.

1a
ADX (Average Directional Index) Filter

Only enter when ADX > 25 (trending market). ADX < 20 indicates range-bound conditions.

# Calculate ADX (14-period) plus_dm = max(high - prev_high, 0) minus_dm = max(prev_low - low, 0) plus_di = 100 * smoothed(plus_dm) / ATR minus_di = 100 * smoothed(minus_dm) / ATR ADX = 100 * smoothed(abs(plus_di - minus_di) / (plus_di + minus_di)) # Entry filter addition if ADX < 20: skip_entry = True # Market is range-bound

Expected Impact: Would have avoided ~6 of the 13-trade streak entries (March-April 2024 range)

1b
Bollinger Band Width Filter

Measure BB width relative to historical average. When BB width is contracting (< 50th percentile), market is likely range-bound.

bb_width = (upper_band - lower_band) / sma20 bb_width_percentile = rolling_percentile(bb_width, period=100) # Adaptive entry based on BB width if bb_width_percentile < 30: min_pos_long = 0.75 # Require stronger positioning in tight range elif bb_width_percentile > 70: min_pos_long = 0.4 # Normal threshold in trending market

Solution 2: Adaptive ATR Stop Loss

Purpose: Address the 25% of streaks caused by ATR lagging actual volatility.

2a
Short-Term vs Long-Term ATR Comparison

Use a blend of short-term (5-period) and long-term (14-period) ATR for stop loss calculation.

atr_short = ATR(5) # Recent volatility atr_long = ATR(14) # Historical volatility # Adaptive ATR: use the lower of the two atr_adaptive = min(atr_short, atr_long) # Alternative: weighted average favoring recent atr_adaptive = 0.7 * atr_short + 0.3 * atr_long

Expected Impact: Tighter stops in low-volatility periods, reducing whipsaw losses

2b
Volatility Regime-Based SL Multiplier

Adjust SL multiplier based on current volatility relative to recent history.

vol_ratio = atr_short / atr_long if vol_ratio < 0.7: # Volatility contracting sl_mult = 1.2 # Tighter stop (was 1.8) elif vol_ratio > 1.3: # Volatility expanding sl_mult = 2.2 # Wider stop (was 1.8) else: sl_mult = 1.8 # Normal

Solution 3: Trend Confirmation Filters

Purpose: Address the 30% of streaks caused by entering during trend reversals.

3a
Higher Highs/Lower Lows Filter

Only enter longs when price is making higher highs AND higher lows (not just above SMA200).

swing_high_1 = rolling_max(high, 20) swing_high_2 = rolling_max(high, 20).shift(20) swing_low_1 = rolling_min(low, 20) swing_low_2 = rolling_min(low, 20).shift(20) making_higher_highs = swing_high_1 > swing_high_2 making_higher_lows = swing_low_1 > swing_low_2 # Require both for entry trend_confirmed = making_higher_highs and making_higher_lows

Expected Impact: Would have blocked most Jun-Jul 2024 entries (lower highs pattern)

3b
Multi-Timeframe Trend Confirmation

Require uptrend on both 15-minute AND 4-hour timeframes.

# 15-minute trend (existing) uptrend_15m = close > SMA(200) # 4-hour trend (resample to 16-bar lookback) close_4h = resample(close, '4H') sma50_4h = SMA(close_4h, 50) # ~8 days on 4H uptrend_4h = close_4h > sma50_4h # Both must be true entry_allowed = uptrend_15m and uptrend_4h
